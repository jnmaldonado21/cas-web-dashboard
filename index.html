On AI
AI makes our web work more important, not less. When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity about a CAS department, degree program, or researcher — the answer comes from our websites. If those pages are broken, outdated, or inaccessible, AI spreads that misinformation to prospective students and collaborators. We make sure AI gets the right story about the College of Arts & Sciences.

Why this work matters to the College
Brand awareness — Every Google search that surfaces a CAS department is a brand impression we didn't pay for.
Enrollment pipeline — The website is the top of the funnel for every prospective student considering any CAS program.
Reputation — Broken, inaccessible, or outdated pages signal that the College doesn't take quality seriously.
AI visibility — ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI pull from our pages. We control what they say about CAS.
