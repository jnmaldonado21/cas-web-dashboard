On AI
AI makes our web work more important, not less. When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity about COAS departments, degree programs, or researchers — the answer comes from our websites. If those pages are broken, outdated, or inaccessible, AI spreads that misinformation to prospective students and collaborators. We make sure AI gets the right story about the College of Arts & Sciences.
